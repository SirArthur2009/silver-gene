Silver Genes

Labrador Retrievers can be registered on AKC in three different colors. Black, chocolate, and yellow, while there are only three recognized colors, there are about seven colors in reality. These colors are black, charcoal, chocolate, silver, yellow, fox red, and white. The charcoal is registered as black, the silver as chocolate, the white and fox red as yellow. Of the aforemention colors, today I want to focus on silver.
